PRODUCT SALE

A product sale refers to the process of exchanging a product or service for money. It involves a transaction between a buyer and a seller where the buyer agrees to pay a certain price for a product that the seller offers. The product sale process can occur in various settings, such as in a physical store, online, over the phone, or through other sales channels..
